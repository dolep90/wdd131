Abidjan, Côte D'ivoire

Ivory Coast is a West African country with seaside resorts, rainforests, and a French colonial heritage. Abidjan, on the Atlantic coast, is the country's main urban center. Its modern sites include the Pyramid, a concrete edifice reminiscent of a ziggurat. St. Paul's Cathedral is a leaning structure attached to a huge cross. North of the central business district, Banco National Park is a rainforest reserve with winding hiking trails. .
